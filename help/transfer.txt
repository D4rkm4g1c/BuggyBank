How to Transfer Funds

Transferring money with BuggyBank is quick and easy!

Step-by-Step Guide:
1. Navigate to the "Transfer Funds" page
2. Enter the recipient's account number
3. Specify the amount you want to transfer
4. Add an optional note (supports HTML formatting)
5. Click "Transfer Funds" to complete the transaction

Important Notes:
- Transfers are processed immediately
- Transfer limits apply based on account type
- Notes support plain text formatting
- CSRF protection is implemented

Security Features:
- All transfers are logged for audit purposes
- Transaction history is available in your dashboard
- Support team can assist with any issues

For questions about transfers, please contact our support team.
